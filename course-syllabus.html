A. COURSE DESCRIPTION

MKTG 240 SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

Studies the applications and methods utilized to promote businesses and organizations via digital tools and social media. Includes interface uses and best practices. Students will create and manage various social media sites while completing writing and reading assignments, quizzes, and a learning portfolio. The course also includes an overview of how to use social media platforms to supplement traditional marketing strategies.

  • Recommended prerequisite: Basic keyboarding, Internet, and computer skills.
  • Requirement: Students must be able to access and interact with various social media websites, including but not limited to Facebook, Flickr, YouTube, Linkedin, X, TikTok, etc.
  • Prerequisite: None. (PCS 1.1, 3 credit hours: 3 hours lecture, 0 hours lab)

B. LEARNING OBJECTIVES

Upon successful completion of the course, a student should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate an understanding of a variety of social media spaces
  2. Implement social media tools across platforms
  3. Identify effective marketing strategies through social media usage
  4. Utilize messaging and images to further marketing goals via social media
  5. Contribute to a blog for personal or professional purposes
